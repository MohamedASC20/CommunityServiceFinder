How To Log Community Service Hours:

- Whether its for school or work, we want to make your experience as seamless as possible.

- After completing an event, find the link at the bottom of this page to the editable PDF that allows you to to enter your information.

- After filling out the information, print the document and have it signed by the supervisors of their corresponding events.

- Hand in the document to wherever you need your hours logged and enjoy the satisfaction of helping your community!
